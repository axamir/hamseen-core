# MVP Task Breakdown

## M0 — Documentation gate
Resolve stakeholder unknowns, approve MVP, workflows, screens, design, and technical decisions.

## M1 — Local demonstrator
Monorepo, design system, Persian localization, landing page, synthetic Lavizan data, resident hub, guest approval, guard queue, account summary, ticket, and reservation.

## M2 — Platform foundation
Authentication, tenant context, RBAC, audit log, PostgreSQL migrations, files, notifications, observability, CI.

## M3 — Operational MVP
Units/occupancy, announcements, guests, packages, tickets, reservations, management explorer.

## M4 — Financial MVP
Charge periods, unit ledger, receipt review, expenses, statements, reconciliation controls.

## M5 — Channels and controlled AI
Push, one pilot bot, AI Gateway, FAQ, classification, summaries, approval log.

## M6 — Pilot readiness
Imports, training, support, backups, security/accessibility/performance reviews, pilot analytics and acceptance rehearsal.

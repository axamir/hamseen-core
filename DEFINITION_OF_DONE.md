# Definition of Done

A feature is done only when:

- Its scope maps to documented workflow and screen IDs.
- Authorization, tenant isolation, validation, audit, and notification behavior are implemented.
- Loading, empty, error, offline, forbidden, success, and confirmation states are addressed.
- Persian RTL and English LTR work on mobile and desktop.
- Unit/integration tests pass; critical journeys have Playwright coverage.
- Accessibility and performance checks meet agreed budgets.
- API/schema migrations are reviewed and reversible.
- No credentials or real personal data are present.
- Documentation and screenshots are current.
- Product acceptance criteria are demonstrably satisfied.

# Project Metadata and Governance

## Bilingual policy

- Numbered files in `docs/en` and `docs/fa` must have identical filenames.
- Product decisions must remain semantically aligned.
- A change to one language requires the matching change in the other before merge.

## Writing policy

- Distinguish confirmed facts, hypotheses, proposals, and future scope.
- Do not claim AI, ROI, legal compliance, security certification, or pilot success without evidence.
- Use English technical identifiers consistently; explain them in Persian where helpful.

## Change policy

- Material scope and architecture decisions require a documented rationale.
- Security, finance, identity, permission, and privacy changes require specialist review.
- Never commit credentials, resident data, production exports, or personally identifiable pilot records.

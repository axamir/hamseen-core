# 10 — Marketplace and Community

## Professional directory

Optional resident cards contain public display name, specialty, skills, service area, availability, portfolio/contact controls, and verification badges. Unit number is private by default and may never be exposed merely because a profile is searchable.

## Marketplace

Listings support product/service type, images, description, price, quantity or schedule, fulfillment, order status, reviews, moderation, and dispute reporting. Daily limited inventory supports ghost kitchens and home producers.

## Community

Interest groups, events, volunteering, charity markets, parent groups, elder support, sports, books, walking, and learning. Users control discoverability and messaging.

## Safety

Prohibited categories, moderation, reporting, age safeguards, lawful home-business rules, allergen/food disclosures, and clear separation between platform facilitation and provider responsibility.

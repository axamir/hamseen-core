# 18 — Build Order

1. **Decision lock:** approve MVP scope, terminology, permission matrix, UX flows.
2. **Foundation:** monorepo, CI, environments, observability, design tokens, localization.
3. **Identity and tenancy:** auth, community membership, roles, audit.
4. **Building graph:** Lavizan block/wing/floor/unit and occupancy import.
5. **Shared hub:** app shell, navigation, search, notifications, preferences.
6. **Operations:** announcements, guest, package, tickets, attachments, assignments.
7. **Finance:** accounts, charges, receipts, expenses, statements, controls.
8. **Reservations:** amenity policies, availability, booking, cancellation.
9. **Channels:** push plus one of Bale/Telegram, then the other.
10. **Basic AI:** controlled FAQ, classification, summaries through AI Gateway.
11. **Pilot tooling:** onboarding, imports, support, analytics, feedback, incident playbooks.
12. **Hardening:** performance, security, accessibility, backup restore, disaster rehearsal.

No marketplace, native app, predictive AI, or broad commercialization work may block the pilot core.

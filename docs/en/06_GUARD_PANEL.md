# 06 — Guard Panel

The guard uses the same hub with a focused operations app optimized for a tablet and unreliable connectivity.

## Workspace

- Expected, waiting, approved, rejected, entered, and exited visitors
- Quick package registration and unclaimed-package queue
- Contractor permits and expiration time
- Emergency contacts and operational notices
- Shift handover, incidents, and unresolved items

## Visitor flow

1. Search pre-registered invitation or record walk-in details.
2. Notify the host through app, Telegram/Bale preference, then fallback channel.
3. Show clear decision and allowed time.
4. Record entry and exit with operator, gate, and timestamp.
5. Escalate no-response according to community policy.

## Package flow

Scan or enter carrier and unit, optionally photograph the label, notify resident, record collection, and highlight overdue packages.

## Safety

No financial balances, private resident messages, or unrestricted resident exports. Offline actions queue with visible sync status and conflict handling.

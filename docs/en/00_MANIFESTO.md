# 00 — Manifesto

Residential buildings are physical infrastructure; communities are living systems. Hamseen gives that living system a shared digital layer.

Hamseen is not merely a charge-payment app or an administrative dashboard. It is a Community Operating System where residents, staff, management, and service providers share one familiar interface while receiving different capabilities through permissions.

## Principles

1. **Good neighbors are a blessing.** Technology should strengthen trust and cooperation.
2. **One community, one source of truth.** Operational facts must not be scattered across calls, paper, and chat groups.
3. **Familiar before clever.** The experience should feel like a mobile app or Telegram mini app, not enterprise software.
4. **Modules, not clutter.** Every capability behaves like an installable mini app.
5. **Privacy by role.** Users and AI see only what their role permits.
6. **Human authority.** AI may summarize, draft, and recommend; sensitive action requires human approval.
7. **Pilot before scale.** Lavizan validates behavior before commercial rollout.

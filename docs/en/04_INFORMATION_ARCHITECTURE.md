## Navigation rules

- Mobile-first home screen with recognizable app tiles.
- Three taps or fewer for high-frequency actions.
- Actionable notifications open the exact task, not a generic inbox.
- Mini apps share navigation, identity, messaging, payments, and permissions.
- Users may pin, hide, and reorder eligible mini apps.

# 08 — Accounting and Reserve Fund

## Scope

- Charge rules by unit, area, occupancy, service, or approved formula
- Period generation, adjustments, exemptions, penalties where lawful
- Resident ledger: opening balance, charges, credits, payments, refunds
- Receipt upload, review, approval, rejection, and reason
- Expenses, vendors, invoices, payroll records, contracts, and cost centers
- Income beyond charges and dedicated reserve-fund ledger
- Cash/bank reconciliation and immutable posting history

## Controls

- Double-entry accounting is preferred for production.
- Posted entries are reversed, not silently edited or deleted.
- Maker-checker approval for sensitive transactions.
- Attach source documents and preserve audit history.
- Currency, calendar, tax, and legal behavior are configurable and require local professional validation.

## Reports

Unit statement, receivables aging, collection rate, income/expense statement, budget variance, vendor spend, payroll summary, contract commitments, reserve balance and movements.

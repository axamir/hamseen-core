# 15 — Security and Privacy

## Baseline

- Tenant isolation enforced in application and database policy
- MFA for privileged roles; secure session rotation and revocation
- Encryption in transit and at rest; secrets in a managed vault
- Least privilege, field-level masking, and short-lived service credentials
- Immutable audit trail for access and sensitive change
- Encrypted backups, tested restore, retention and deletion schedules
- Dependency, code, infrastructure, and penetration testing before production

## Personal data

Collect only necessary data, explain purpose, record consent where required, provide correction/export processes, and avoid exposing unit address through social features. Define retention for visitors, access logs, packages, messages, finance, and documents with legal counsel.

## Incident readiness

Severity levels, on-call ownership, containment, evidence preservation, notification process, recovery objectives, and post-incident review.

## Important

This document is a product baseline, not legal or security certification. Iranian deployment and later UAE deployment require separate legal, privacy, payment, and hosting reviews.

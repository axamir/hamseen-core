# 16 — MVP Scope

## Goal

Prove that one shared system improves daily operations and resident trust in a controlled Lavizan pilot.

## Included

- Tenant/community setup and Lavizan hierarchy
- Authentication, membership, roles, permissions, audit log
- Mobile-first resident hub
- Unit and occupancy records
- Announcements and notification preferences
- Guest approval and package tracking
- Tickets, attachments, assignment, status, suggestions/complaints
- Basic amenity reservation
- Basic accounting: charge, balance, receipt review, expense, statement
- Guard, manager, accountant, and resident workspaces
- PWA plus one bot integration selected for pilot
- Scripted/basic AI: FAQ, ticket classification, notice summary; no autonomous execution

## Excluded from MVP

Full marketplace, property transactions, native apps, IoT, predictive maintenance, comprehensive payroll, autonomous AI, multi-country compliance, and public social network.

## Success signals

High completion of guest/package and ticket workflows, reduced phone/manual follow-up, reliable financial reconciliation, weekly active households, and positive usability across age groups.

# 02 — Lavizan Pilot

## Site

Lavizan residential complex, Mashhad, Bolvar Namaz, end of Delavaran Boulevard. The site overlooks mountain and park scenery and is described by stakeholders as approximately 200 meters above the Imam Reza shrine elevation.

## Structure

- Blocks: A, B, C
- Floors per block: 14
- Units per floor: 8
- Wings per floor: 2, with 4 units in each wing
- Units per block: 112
- Total units: 336

Example: `A1-01` through `A1-04` are floor 1, wing 1, block A; `A1-05` through `A1-08` are floor 1, wing 2.

## Known amenities

- Football and basketball court
- Children’s playground
- Community hall
- Rooftop cafe

## Pilot hypothesis

A unified resident hub plus guest/package, announcements, tickets, reservations, unit records, and basic accounting can reduce operational friction and increase trust.

## Rollout

Start with 20–40 units, one guard shift, one manager, and limited accounting data. Expand to one block after stability, then all three blocks.

## Unknowns to validate

Legal entity, contact information, social accounts, current accounting process, resident demographics, existing service contracts, internet coverage, payment provider, and formal approval authority.

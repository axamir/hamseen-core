# 25 — Presentation Landing Page

## Goal

Let a resident, board member, developer, or investor understand Hamseen patiently as an interactive catalog without requiring a live sales meeting.

## Story

1. Hero: Hamseen, “Good neighbors are a blessing.”
2. The familiar problem: calls, paper, scattered chats, unclear follow-up.
3. The promise: one community, one familiar hub.
4. Lavizan digital twin overview: 3 blocks, 336 units, amenities.
5. A day with Hamseen: guest, package, charge, ticket, reservation, cafe.
6. Role worlds: resident, guard, manager, accountant, provider.
7. Community economy: professionals, home businesses, marketplace.
8. Governance and transparency.
9. AI Intelligence Layer with permission and human approval.
10. Security and privacy principles.
11. Pilot roadmap and what is/not yet built.
12. Participation CTA: resident feedback, pilot partner, provider, investor/contact.

## Experience

Mobile-first Persian default with English switch, sticky chapter navigation, interactive phone mockups, role switcher, before/after comparison, accessible motion, fast loading, shareable section URLs, printable summary, analytics with consent, and no fabricated testimonials or ROI.

## Conversion events

`view_role_demo`, `open_feature`, `switch_language`, `download_summary`, `submit_interest`, `submit_feedback`, each privacy-safe and documented.

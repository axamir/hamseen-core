# 26 — Board Data Validation Questionnaire

## Purpose and use

This is the official collection and approval form for Lavizan pilot data. A printed or PDF copy is completed by the board, internal manager, accountant, and guard supervisor. Answers are then recorded in this file; later changes retain the date, approver, and decision source.

The form separates three data states:

- **Confirmed:** an authorized stakeholder has verified the value.
- **Proposed default:** a Hamseen standard proposal, not a Lavizan fact until approved.
- **Answer required:** must not be represented as a building fact or public claim without stakeholder input.

> Data rule: estimates, examples, and defaults must never be published as building facts, financial figures, legal policies, or public claims.

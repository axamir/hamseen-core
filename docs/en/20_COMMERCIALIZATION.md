# 20 — Commercialization

## Product model

Multi-tenant SaaS with a shared core and community-specific branding, configuration, data isolation, modules, and policies.

## Potential revenue

- Setup, migration, and training fee
- Monthly/annual subscription by units and enabled modules
- Premium operations, accounting, AI, analytics, and support tiers
- Approved provider commission or software fee
- White-label and enterprise integration

No financial projection or ROI is guaranteed before pilot evidence.

## Packaging

- **Core:** units, residents, notices, guests, packages, tickets
- **Operations:** reservations, staff, providers, contracts, reports
- **Finance:** charges, ledger, receipts, expenses, reserve fund
- **Community:** profiles, marketplace, groups, voting, property
- **Intelligence:** smart inbox, role assistants, analytics

## Commercial readiness

Tenant provisioning, billing, SLA, support model, onboarding playbook, data migration, monitoring, compliance pack, partner contracts, product analytics, demo environment, and case study.

## Go-to-market

Use Lavizan as a documented design partner, not a claimed success before measured results. Target managed residential complexes, developers, property managers, and smart-community programs.

# 01 — Product Overview

Hamseen digitizes four connected layers of residential life:

1. **Operations:** units, occupancy, guests, packages, tickets, notices, reservations.
2. **Governance and finance:** charges, payments, expenses, contracts, reserve fund, voting, audit trails.
3. **Services and commerce:** cafe, grocery, laundry, car wash, home services, resident marketplace, property listings.
4. **Community and intelligence:** professional profiles, groups, campaigns, surveys, smart inbox, reporting, role assistants.

## Users

Residents, owners, tenants, guards, internal managers, accountants, board members, super administrators, employees, and service providers.

## Value

- Residents gain one simple entry point to community life.
- Staff replace fragmented calls and paper with traceable workflows.
- Boards gain financial and operational visibility.
- Providers gain a trusted local demand channel.
- Properties gain a more modern, service-rich living proposition.

## Not in scope

Hamseen is not a banking system, legal authority, emergency service, or autonomous decision-maker. It integrates with specialized providers and preserves accountable human control.

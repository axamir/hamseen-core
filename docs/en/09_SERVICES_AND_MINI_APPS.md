# 09 — Services and Mini Apps

Hamseen Store enables administrators to activate modules without redesigning the hub.

## Initial modules

Cafe, grocery, laundry, car wash, home services, technical maintenance, community hall, sports courts, pool/gym when available, concierge shopping, property, professional directory, marketplace, kids club, and library.

## Shared contract

Every service mini app reuses identity, unit address, catalog, availability, order/reservation, status timeline, messaging, notification, payment/settlement, cancellation, rating, dispute, and audit components.

## Provider workspace

Catalog and prices, inventory/capacity, opening hours, incoming orders, acceptance or rejection, fulfillment status, resident messaging, refunds where authorized, settlement, analytics, and staff permissions.

## Governance

Management approves providers, categories, commission, service area, privacy access, complaint policy, and suspension. Home businesses require transparent labeling and compliance with building rules and applicable law.

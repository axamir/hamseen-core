# 19 — Pilot Plan

## Stage 0 — Discovery

Interview board, manager, accountant, guards, owners, tenants, older residents, younger residents, and providers. Map current tools, exceptions, trust concerns, and legal authority.

## Stage 1 — Prototype

Clickable mobile resident, guard tablet, management desktop, and accounting flows. Test language, navigation, guest response, receipt review, ticket status, and reservation.

## Stage 2 — Controlled live pilot

20–40 units, one guard shift, one manager, selected accounting period, and one amenity. Parallel-run critical records with the existing process until reconciliation passes.

## Stage 3 — One block

Train users, migrate verified unit data, operate support desk, measure adoption and error rates, and conduct weekly review.

## Stage 4 — Full complex

Roll out blocks A, B, C in waves. Enable additional services only after operational core stability.

## Metrics

Activation, weekly active households, guest approval time, package collection time, ticket resolution, receipt review time, collection reconciliation, support volume, satisfaction, and critical incident count.

## Exit criteria

Stable critical flows, reconciled accounting, approved privacy/security review, documented support ownership, trained staff, and board acceptance.

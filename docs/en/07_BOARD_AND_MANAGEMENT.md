# 07 — Board and Management

## Community map

Navigate block → wing → floor → unit. Each unit record shows authorized identity, ownership/occupancy periods, household members, vehicles, operational tickets, financial summary, documents, and audit history according to permission.

## Operations

- Assign and prioritize tickets
- Publish public, block, floor, role, or unit notices
- Manage staff, providers, amenity rules, schedules, and capacity
- Track contracts, expirations, tasks, incidents, and service levels
- Review suggestions, complaints, surveys, and formal votes
- Configure mini apps and role access

## Governance

Draft agendas, attach documents, record quorum, collect eligible votes, publish decisions, and preserve immutable result history. Digital voting must follow applicable law and community bylaws.

## Reporting

Daily operations brief, open-ticket aging, recurring issues, collection rate, expense variance, reserve position, provider performance, resident participation, and privacy-safe adoption metrics.
